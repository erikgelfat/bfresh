About

B-FRESH network was established in 2014, The chain specializes in the sale of health drinks in a unique preparation.
We use products in exclusive development and production, in special preparation methods and machines developed to provide innovation to the Israeli public and a unique and healthy drinking experience.
The category of health drinks in Israel has gained momentum in recent years due to the increasing awareness among the population of the consumption of essential beverages for our bodies and their integration into daily life, as a substitute for a light meal, between meals or as drinks for energy, vitamins and refreshment.


The network was founded with the intention of meeting these needs, with an emphasis on healthy energy, freshness and naturalness.
The network developed together with an expert chef, nutritionist and food technologist recipes that fit the entire population, from preschool through the age of retirement, a combination of pampering and health - a drink that gives strength.
In our menu you will find drinks based on yogurt, green tea, coconut milk, sorbet, fruit and vegetable juices and combinations of dried fruit, nuts, honey and energy-filled ingredients.
All the drinks will be complemented by tapioca pearls and fruit balls that are unique to us.
